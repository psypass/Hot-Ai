2. **FaceCam: Portrait Video Camera Control via Scale-Aware Condi**
   - 摘要:

   - [论文链接](http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05506v1)

3. **RoboPocket: Improve Robot Policies Instantly with Your Phone**
   - 摘要:

   - [论文链接](http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05504v1)
